Recipe #3:American Sushi

  • Time: about 2 hours
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
bacon 12 strips
veal 1/4 pound
beef 1 pound
breadcrumbs 1/2 cup

Step 1: Freeze half of the beef for at least 12 hours, and then refrigerate for 5

Step 2: Cut beef into cubes for tartar

Step 3:form your veal, breadcrumbs, and remaining beef into balls

Step 4:put bacon strips and then meatballs into metal rings, and create a hole in the middle of the meatball so you can see through them, forming a ring of meat

Step 5:cook on a preheated cast iron skillet

Step 6: Put the tartar into the middle of the ring

Chef's Tip: if you want to get really american, deep fry the whole thing!
